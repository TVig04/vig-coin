# Vig Coin (VIG)

Vig Coin (VIG) is an ERC-20 token deployed on the Base network.

## Overview

Vig Coin was created as a fixed-supply digital asset designed for experimentation, on-chain development, and real-world payment exploration. The total supply of 1,000,000,000 VIG was minted at deployment, with no built-in transfer restrictions.

## Vision & Utility

The long-term objective of Vig Coin is to explore integration within online gaming and sportsbook ecosystems as a digital payment option.

In parallel, VIG is designed to be freely transferable and tradable on compatible decentralized platforms, allowing open market participation.

The project is currently in early-stage development and evaluation, with a focus on building infrastructure and assessing compliant pathways for future real-world use cases.
